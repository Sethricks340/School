notes for social media SWOT analysis

Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
(first two are internal, organization can control, second are external cant control)

strengths
* what is the company doing well on social media
	* brand
	* market penetration
	* reach, how many people
	* content 

weaknesses 
* where could improve on social media
	* content 
	* scale or scope  
	* social media community 
	* everything under strengths could be a weakness

opportunities
* what is happening in the environment?
* new platforms (seasonality)

threats
* what is happening in he environment
* crisis events
* new competition
