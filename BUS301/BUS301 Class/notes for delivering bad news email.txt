* help readers understand and accept the bad news
* maintain trust for the sake of the business relationship
* reduce anxiety and frustration about the situation

Procedure video notes: 

* procedure documents reduce human error, step-by-step process
* task can be repeated by different people (a recipe)
* can include pictures and other media
* may require completing the task a few times
* create a procedure for only one process
* write steps in the order they occur
* keep each step short but clear
* keep writing easy to understand, 4th grade reading level
* include lists or bullet points as needed
